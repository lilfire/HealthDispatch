# HealthDispatch Logo Suggestions

## Concept A: Medical Cross + Signal Waves

**File:** `concept-a-medical-cross-signal.svg`
**Android drawables:** `concept_a_foreground.xml` + `concept_a_background.xml`

- **Colors:** Deep green gradient (#1B8A5A to #0D6B3F)
- **Design:** White medical cross centered on a green circle, with signal/broadcast waves radiating from the right side
- **Symbolism:** The cross represents healthcare; the signal waves represent dispatch/transmission of health data
- **Feel:** Professional, medical, trustworthy

## Concept B: Heart + Forward Arrow with Pulse Line

**File:** `concept-b-heart-arrow.svg`
**Android drawables:** `concept_b_foreground.xml` + `concept_b_background.xml`

- **Colors:** Blue gradient (#2196F3 to #1565C0)
- **Design:** White heart shape on a blue rounded square, with a forward/play arrow inside the heart, and a pulse/heartbeat line at the bottom
- **Symbolism:** The heart represents health/care; the arrow represents dispatch/sending; the pulse line reinforces the health monitoring theme
- **Feel:** Modern, dynamic, approachable

## How to Apply

To use a concept as the app launcher icon, copy the foreground and background XML files to:

- `app/src/main/res/drawable/ic_launcher_foreground.xml`
- `app/src/main/res/drawable/ic_launcher_background.xml`
